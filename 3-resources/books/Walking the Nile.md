## Highlights
> Umuganda, Boston explained, was a custom particular to Kigali itself. On the last Saturday of every month, the entire population of Kigali is required to devote itself to the city’s upkeep. For one day a month, business in Kigali comes to a stand-still and every man, woman and child turns out to sweep the city’s streets, tend its parklands and hedges.
